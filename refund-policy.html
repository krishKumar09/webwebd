Returns & Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Satisfaction Policy (No Refunds)

Our services are non-refundable. If you are not fully satisfied with the quality of your cleaning, you must notify our main office within 24 hours. We will promptly return to address and correct the issue at no additional charge. This remedy is exclusive and replaces any right to a refund or other compensation.

We Make It Right

At Maids of Cy-Fair, your satisfaction is our priority. While we do not offer refunds, we stand behind our work. If you're not happy with your cleaning:

  • Contact us within 24 hours of your service
  • We'll come back out to address any concerns
  • No additional charge for the re-clean

Cancellation Policy

Late Cancellation Fee: $100

Cancellations made less than 24 hours before your scheduled appointment will incur a $100 cancellation fee.

We understand that life happens. To avoid the late cancellation fee, please notify us at least 24 hours in advance if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment.

  • Cancellations (less than 24 hours): $100 fee
  • Rescheduling (less than 24 hours): $25 fee

Refund Policy

If you are not satisfied with the service, please contact us at info@maidsofcyfair.com. We'll do our best to address your concerns and make things right, but refunds are not guaranteed.

Our commitment is to provide excellent service and we prefer to resolve any issues by returning to your home to complete any missed areas or address any concerns you may have.

Why We Offer Re-Cleans Instead of Refunds

As a service-based business, our team dedicates time, effort, and resources to every cleaning appointment. By offering to return and re-clean rather than providing refunds, we can:

  • Ensure you receive the quality clean you deserve
  • Demonstrate our commitment to customer satisfaction
  • Continue improving our services based on your feedback
